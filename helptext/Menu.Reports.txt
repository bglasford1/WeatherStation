Main Menu -> Reports:

The Reports option of the main menu contains sub-menu options that generate reports.  The reports are very similar to what is generated by the Davis software. Some variations have been made.  The sub-menu options are listed below along with their function.

Sunrise/Sunset Report:  Generates a table of sunrise/sunset times plus other times.

Bright Sunshine Report:  Generates the hours of bright sunshine for each day selected.

NOAA Monthly Report:  Generates a monthly report of various key values by day.

NOAA Yearly Report:  Generates a yearly report of various key values by month.
